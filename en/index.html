FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

👟

My steps aren't showing up

Make sure step reading is allowed under Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Strimo on your iPhone. If you use an Apple Watch, it also counts steps on its own.

💾

My data is gone / "recovery mode"

If data fails to load, the app starts in a temporary recovery mode. Your previous data is automatically backed up. Restart the app to retry opening in normal mode.

💳

Managing or cancelling a subscription

Manage or cancel the monthly pass under Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. One-time purchases (Remove Ads, Full Editor) stay active permanently after purchase.

📡

Nearby (pass-by) isn't working

Pass-by encounters need Bluetooth and Location permissions, plus another player nearby. Allow these permissions and keep the app open to try.

🎨

About AI image generation

AI generation is available up to 3 times per day (1st free, 2nd–3rd via a rewarded ad). Rights to the images you generate belong to you.

🗑️

I want to delete my account

You can delete it from Settings > Account in the app. Deletion permanently removes all data, including data on the server, and cannot be undone.
